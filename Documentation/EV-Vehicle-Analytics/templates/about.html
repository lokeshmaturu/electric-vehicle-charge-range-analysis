About EV Analytics

Electric Vehicle Charge & Range Analysis Platform

EV Analytics is a data visualization platform developed to analyze Electric Vehicle charging patterns, battery performance, driving range, and vehicle efficiency using MySQL, Tableau, and Flask.

The platform transforms raw EV datasets into meaningful dashboards and interactive stories, enabling users to make informed decisions through visual analytics.

Project Overview

A complete business intelligence solution for Electric Vehicle data visualization and analysis.

What is EV Analytics?

EV Analytics is an interactive web application that combines database management, Tableau dashboards, and Flask to present electric vehicle information through visually rich dashboards and stories.

The platform enables users to explore charging infrastructure, battery specifications, vehicle performance, and driving range using interactive filters and KPIs.

Project Goal

To develop a responsive visualization platform that simplifies Electric Vehicle analysis for students, researchers, consumers, and decision makers.

The system converts complex datasets into meaningful insights through Tableau dashboards integrated into a Flask web application.

Our Mission

To provide a modern visualization platform that enables users to understand electric vehicle data through interactive dashboards, meaningful KPIs, and user-friendly analytics.

Our Vision

To support the adoption of sustainable transportation by providing intelligent insights into EV charging, battery performance, and vehicle efficiency through modern business intelligence tools.
